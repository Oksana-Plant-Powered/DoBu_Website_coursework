Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about DoBu Martial Arts.

Ask something else

What is DoBu Martial Arts?

DoBu Martial Arts is a comprehensive training center offering classes in various martial arts disciplines including Jiu-jitsu, Karate, Judo, and Muay Thai. Our mission is to provide high-quality martial arts training to students of all ages and skill levels.

What classes do you offer?

We offer a variety of classes including Jiu-jitsu, Karate, Judo, Muay Thai, and specialized classes for kids. We also provide private tuition and open mat sessions for personal practice.

How do I sign up for a class?

Signing up for a class is easy! You can visit our Classes page, choose the class you’re interested in, and follow the booking instructions. Alternatively, you can contact us directly for assistance.

What should I wear to my first class?

For your first class, we recommend wearing comfortable athletic wear that allows for movement. If you continue with classes, specific uniforms or gear may be required depending on the martial art you are practicing.

Do I need any prior experience?

No prior experience is necessary to join our classes. We welcome beginners as well as advanced practitioners. Our instructors will tailor the training to match your skill level.

Are there classes for children?

Yes, we offer a range of classes specifically designed for children. These classes focus on building confidence, discipline, and physical fitness in a fun and supportive environment.

What are the membership fees?

Our membership fees vary depending on the type of class and the duration of the membership. For detailed information, please visit our Membership page or contact us directly.

Can I try a class before committing to a membership?

Yes, we offer trial classes for new students. This allows you to experience our training methods and facilities before making a commitment. Please contact us to schedule a trial class.

How can I contact DoBu Martial Arts?

You can contact us via email, phone, or by visiting our location. Our contact details are available on the Contact page. We are always happy to answer any questions you may have.

Do you offer private lessons?

Yes, we offer private tuition for individuals seeking personalized training. Our instructors will work with you to develop a customized training plan that meets your specific goals and needs.
