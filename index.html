Our Classes

Jiu-jitsu

Jiu-jitsu is a martial art that focuses on grappling and ground fighting. Our classes teach techniques for self-defense and competition, helping you build physical strength and mental resilience.

Karate

Karate emphasizes striking techniques such as punching, kicking, and blocking. Our karate classes improve coordination, discipline, and confidence for all ages and skill levels.

Judo

Judo focuses on throws and takedowns. Our judo classes teach balance, leverage, and technique, making it an excellent martial art for self-defense and physical fitness.

Muay Thai

Muay Thai, known as the "art of eight limbs," uses punches, kicks, elbows, and knee strikes. Our classes provide a full-body workout and teach powerful self-defense techniques.

Private Tuition

Our private tuition classes offer personalized training sessions tailored to your individual needs. Perfect for those seeking focused instruction and rapid improvement.

Open Mat/Personal Practice

Open mat sessions allow you to practice techniques and spar with other students in a relaxed environment. Personal practice time helps reinforce your learning and improve skills.

Kids Jiu-jitsu

Our kids jiu-jitsu classes teach young students self-defense, discipline, and respect. Children learn techniques in a fun and supportive environment, promoting physical fitness and confidence.

Kids Judo

Kids judo classes focus on safe and fun instruction, teaching young students the fundamentals of judo. These classes help build strength, coordination, and discipline.

Kids Karate

Our kids karate classes are designed to teach the basics of karate in a fun and engaging way. Children develop self-discipline, confidence, and physical fitness through structured training.

Jiu-jitsu

Our Jiu-jitsu classes at DoBu Martial Arts focus on ground fighting and grappling techniques, emphasizing the importance of leverage and technique over brute strength. Suitable for all skill levels, these classes help in building physical strength, mental resilience, and strategic thinking.

Karate

Karate at DoBu Martial Arts combines traditional techniques with modern training methods. Students learn striking, blocking, and kata (forms) to develop self-discipline, physical fitness, and self-defense skills. Our Karate classes cater to beginners and advanced practitioners alike.

Judo

Judo, known as "the gentle way," focuses on throws, joint locks, and pins. Our Judo classes at DoBu Martial Arts teach the principles of balance, efficiency, and control, making it an excellent martial art for self-defense and physical conditioning.

Muay Thai

Muay Thai, the art of eight limbs, involves the use of fists, elbows, knees, and shins. Our Muay Thai classes at DoBu Martial Arts provide intense cardiovascular workouts while teaching powerful striking techniques. Suitable for all fitness levels, these classes enhance agility, coordination, and strength.

Private Tuition

Our Private Tuition at DoBu Martial Arts offers personalized training sessions tailored to individual needs and goals. Whether you're looking to improve specific techniques, enhance fitness, or prepare for competitions, our private lessons provide focused, one-on-one attention from expert instructors.

Open Mat/Personal Practice

Open Mat sessions at DoBu Martial Arts provide a flexible training environment for personal practice and skill refinement. These sessions allow members to train at their own pace, practice techniques learned in class, and spar with fellow practitioners in a supportive and open setting.

Kids Jiu-jitsu

Our Kids Jiu-jitsu classes at DoBu Martial Arts are designed to be fun, engaging, and educational. These classes teach children the fundamentals of Jiu-jitsu, helping them build confidence, discipline, and physical fitness in a safe and supportive environment.

Kids Judo

Kids Judo classes at DoBu Martial Arts focus on basic throws, holds, and groundwork. These sessions are crafted to be enjoyable and beneficial, helping children develop coordination, strength, and discipline while learning the principles of respect and perseverance.

Kids Karate

Our Kids Karate classes at DoBu Martial Arts offer a structured yet fun approach to martial arts training. Children learn fundamental Karate techniques, develop self-control, and gain a sense of achievement, all while staying active and making new friends.
