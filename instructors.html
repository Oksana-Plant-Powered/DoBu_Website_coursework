Our Instructors

Meet the team of experienced martial arts instructors at DoBu Martial Arts

John Doe

John Doe is a seasoned Jiu-jitsu instructor with over 20 years of experience. He has a deep passion for teaching and is known for his patience and skill in helping students of all levels. John has competed in numerous national and international tournaments, bringing a wealth of knowledge and practical expertise to his classes. His teaching philosophy focuses on discipline, respect, and continuous improvement. John is dedicated to fostering a supportive and challenging environment where students can thrive.

Jane Smith

Jane Smith is an expert Karate instructor with a black belt and over 15 years of teaching experience. She has trained extensively in both traditional and modern Karate techniques and has a passion for sharing her knowledge with students of all ages. Jane's classes are known for their rigorous training and emphasis on self-discipline, physical fitness, and mental resilience. Her dedication to the art of Karate is evident in her students' successes in both local and international competitions.

Mike Brown

Mike Brown is a highly skilled Judo instructor with a deep understanding of the sport. With over 10 years of experience, Mike has trained with some of the top Judo practitioners in the world. His teaching style is focused on developing strong fundamentals, strategic thinking, and physical conditioning. Mike is committed to helping his students achieve their personal best, both on and off the mat. His classes are structured to cater to beginners and advanced students alike, ensuring a comprehensive learning experience.

Sara Lee

Sara Lee is a Muay Thai instructor with a rich background in competitive fighting and training. She has over 12 years of experience in Muay Thai and has won several championships. Sara's approach to teaching is dynamic and energetic, focusing on technique, endurance, and mental toughness. She aims to inspire her students to push their limits and achieve their fitness and martial arts goals. Sara's classes are suitable for all levels, from beginners to advanced fighters, offering a challenging and rewarding experience.

David Kim

David Kim specializes in private tuition, offering personalized training sessions tailored to individual needs and goals. With over 15 years of experience in various martial arts, David provides expert guidance and coaching to help students improve their skills and confidence. His one-on-one sessions are designed to address specific areas of improvement, ensuring rapid progress and mastery of techniques. David's dedication to his students' success is evident in the customized training plans he develops for each individual.

Emily White

Emily White oversees the open mat and personal practice sessions, providing a supportive environment for students to practice and refine their skills. With a background in multiple martial arts disciplines, Emily offers valuable insights and assistance to students during these sessions. Her approach is focused on fostering self-improvement, encouraging exploration of techniques, and promoting a sense of community among practitioners. Emily's dedication to continuous learning and development makes her an invaluable resource for students seeking to enhance their martial arts practice.
